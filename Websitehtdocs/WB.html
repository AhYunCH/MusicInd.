Weyes Blood

Natalie Laura Mering(born June 11, 1988), known professionally as Weyes Blood, is an American singer, songwriter, and musician. Blood has been involved in the underground noise music scene and was briefly bassist of the group Jackie-O Motherfucker and was singer for the band Satanized.

As Weyes Blood she self-released two albums, changing to Weyes Blood to release The Outside Room (2011) on Not Not Fun Records. She then signed with independent label Mexican Summer, through which she released The Innocent (2014) and Front Row Seat To Earth (2016). Her latest studio album, Titanic Rising (2019) was released on Sub Pop, to critical acclaim.
