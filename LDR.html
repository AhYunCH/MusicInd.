Lana Del Rey

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, professionally known as Lana Del Rey was born in New York City, New York, on June 21, 1985. She released her major-label debut,Born to Die, on January 27, 2012. Its singles, including "Blue Jeans" and "National Anthem". The album was a commercial success, being one of only four albums released by a female artist to have spent more than 300 weeks on the Billboard 200 chart. She re-released Born to Die with 8 new tracks, as well as a standalone EP, Paradise, on November 9, 2012.On June 13, 2014, Del Rey released her sophomore major-label album, Ultraviolence. The album was received warmly by critics who appreciated her shift towards a rawer sound. On September 18, 2015, Del Rey released her third major-label album, Honeymoon, which was preceded by two singles, " High By The Beach" and "Music To Watch Boys To". featuring trap influences and a lush sound that contrasted sharply from her previous album. On July 21, 2017, Del Rey released her fourth major-label album, Lust For Life. The album included singles such as "Love", "Lust for life" (featuring The Weeknd).Norman Fucking Rockwell!, her fifth major-label album, was released on August 30, 2019, to widespread critical acclaim. Her sixth major-label album Chemtrails Over the Country Club was released March 19, 2021, again to critical acclaim. The album saw the release of three singles: "Let Me Love You Like A Woman", "Chemtrails Over The Country Club" and "White Dress".
