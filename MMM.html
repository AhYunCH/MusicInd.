MAMAMOO
Mamamoo commonly stylized and referred to as MAMAMOO, is a South Korean girl group formed under Rainbow Bridge World, consisting of members Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein, and Hwasa.

They debuted on June 19, 2014 with the extended play Hello and the single Mr. Ambiguous. The group is primarily known for their vocal performances and their use of various musical genres. All of the members have their own solo careers.

MAMAMOO released Ahh Oop! on April 2,2015 a collaboration with Esna. On June 19, they released the extended play Pink Funky, which included Ahh Oop!, and the single Um Oh Ah Yeh.

I Miss You and Taller than You were released on January 26 and February 12, 2016 respectively. Both were included in MAMAMOO's first studio album Melting, and the single You're the Best was the title track of it (February 26). On August 31,2016 they released the sub-unit singles Angel and DAB DAB. They released New York on September 21, and the three previously mentioned singles were included in their extended play Memory (November 7, 2016). DÃ©calcomanie was the title track of the EP.

They released the extended play Purple on June 22,2017 with the title track Yes I am. On January 18,2018 MAMAMOO released Paint Me, subsequently starting the Four Seasons, Four Colors project. The first EP of the project was Yellow Flower, and it was released on March 7, 2018 alongside the single Starry Night. The second EP of the project was Red Moon, and it was released on July 16 with Egotistic as its title track. The third installment of the project, BLUE;S, was released on November 29, 2018 with the title track Wind Flower.

On March 14,2019 the Four Seasons, Four Colors project ended with the EP White Wind and the single gogobebe. On November 14,2019 they released the studio album reality in BLACK with the title track HIP. On September 10, MAMAMOO released the advertising single WANNA BE MYSELF for the athleisure brand Andar. MAMAMOO newest comeback is on November 3,2020 with released the EP TRAVEL and the title track AYA. TRAVEL's pre-released single, Dingga, was released on October 20, at 6 PM KST. On February 3, TRAVEL's Japanese edition was be released. The edition contains three bonus tracks. The first bonus track is Dingga -Japanese ver.-, the MV for the single was released on January 20 at 12 AM JST (January 19, 10 PM KST). The second track, AYA -Japanese ver.-, was released on January 27 at 12 AM JST (January 26, 10 PM KST). The third track is Just Believe In Love.
