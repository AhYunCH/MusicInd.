Conan Gray
Conan Lee Gray (born: December 5, 1998) is an American singer-songwriter and social media personality. Conan had started posting on YouTube from a very early age, most of his videos being vlogs, rants, covers of songs and about daily life.

He signed a record deal with Republic Records in 2018, where he released his debut EP Sunset Season (2018). His debut studio album Kid Krow (2020) debuted at number 5 on the US Billboard 200, making it the biggest US new artist debut of 2020. His most successful singles Maniac and Heather where uploaded separately although part of the same album Kid Krow.
