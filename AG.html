Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande-Butera (born June 26, 1993) is an American singer, songwriter and actress.
She began her career in the role of Cat Valentine for the hit Nickelodeon show Victorious and later its spinoff Sam & Cat. In 2013, she released her debut album Yours Truly which debuted
at number one on the Billboard 200. In the following year, Grande's single "Problem" was named
song of the summer, and her second album, My Everything (released less than a year after her first)
also reached number one on the charts.

In 2016, she released her third studio album, Dangerous Woman. In 2018, Grande released her fourth studio album, Sweetener, which topped various charts. Her fifth studio album Thank U, Next , released less than six months after Sweetener, produced two US Billboard Hot 100 number-one singles, "Thank U, Next" and "7 Rings", giving Grande her first number ones in the country.
